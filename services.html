4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY REGISTRATION

A. TRADEMARK REGISTRATION

A Trademark is a symbol or sign which differentiates the goods and services of one business from another. It could be a name, logo, slogan, domain name, shape, colour or sound. A trademark may be registered either plainly (black and white) or in colour. If colour, protection is limited to the registered colour; if plain, protection is unlimited as it applies to all colours of presentation of the trademark.

As an accredited agent, we can help you register your Trademark at the Trademark, Patent and Design Registry, Commercial law department of Nigerian Federal Ministry of Trade and Investment.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the eligibility requirement before it can be registered?

  • It must be distinctive for the goods and services you provide
  • It should not be deceptive, illegal/immoral or similar to existing trademark.

What is the validity/ duration of the trademark?

  • It is valid for 7years
  • Its indefinitely renewable for a period of 14years
  • Application for renewal should be made not less than 3months from due date.

What are the benefits of registering my trademark?

Your trademark is your intellectual property. Once it is registered, it becomes protected from any infringement by ensuring the exclusive right to use it to identify your goods or services. You can take legal action against anyone who uses it without your permission. Your registered trademark is an asset that you can license/assign to a third party to use for a specific period or sell outrightly.

What information/documents do I need to commence registration?

  • Applicant's details
  • Trademark information: scan of the logo which may be words or device/symbol, the name of the brand/trademark you intend to protect.
  • Information about the line of business or type of goods and services you provide.
  • Class of goods proposed to be covered by the trademark: Nigeria presently follows the international classification of goods; a separate application is filed for each classification of goods for which the trademark is to be registered.

What is the procedure for registration?

A preliminary search is conducted to find out the distinctiveness of the trademark from existing and pending applications. After filing the application for trademark registration, the registrar will issue an official acknowledgement letter. If the registrar finds the trademark acceptable for registration, a letter/notice of acceptance will be issued.

Every trademark application must be advertised in the Nigerian Trademark Journal and is open to opposition for a period of 2 months from the date of advertisement. If no objections are received or sustained, the Registrar will issue a certificate of registration.

How long does the process take?

  • Acknowledgment letter= 24hours from filling of application
  • Letter/notice of acceptance= 4-8 weeks from filling of application
  • Publication in Trademark Journal= 32 weeks from acceptance letter stage
  • Grant of certificate= 12 weeks from publication

B. PATENT REGISTRATION

A patent protects new inventions and covers how things work, what they do, how they do it, what they are made of and how they are made. It gives the owner the right to prevent others from making the invention that has been patented or using, importing or selling it without permission.

As an accredited agent, we can help you with the application for a grant of patent for your invention at the Trademark, Patent and Design Registry, Commercial law department of Nigerian Federal Ministry of Trade and Investment.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the eligibility requirement before my invention can be granted a patent?

  • The invention must be new
  • Must have an inventive step that is not obvious to someone with knowledge and experience in the subject
  • Should be capable of being made or used in some kind of industry

IT SHOULD NOT BE:

  • A scientific or mathematical discovery, theory or method
  • A literary, dramatic, musical or artistic work
  • A way of performing a mental act, playing a game or doing business
  • The presentation of information or some computer programs
  • An animal or plant variety
  • A method of medical treatment or diagnosis
  • Against public policy or morality

How long does the process take?

  • Acknowledgment letter= 24hours from filling of application
  • Acceptance letter= 6-8 weeks from filling of application
  • Grant of certificate= 6 weeks from acceptance

What is the duration of the patent

It shall lapse at the end of the 20th year from the date of filling of patent application.

C. DESIGN REGISTRATION

Industrial Design is the aesthetic aspect of an article. It can be any combination of lines or colours or both and any three-dimensional form. It applies to products of Industry and handicrafts such as technical & medical instruments, watches, jewellery, house ware, electrical appliances, vehicles, architectural structures, textile designs, leisure goods and other luxury items. It does not protect any feature of the article, it only protects the design that is aesthetic in nature.

As an accredited agent, we can help you with the registration of your design at the Trademark, Patent and Design Registry, Commercial law department of Nigerian Federal Ministry of Trade and Investment.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What makes my design eligible to be registered?

  • Design must be new or original
  • Industrial design has to be appealing to the eye
  • Design should not be contrary to public order or morality

What are the benefits of registering my industrial design?

The owner of a protected industrial design is granted the right to prevent unauthorized persons from copying, importing, selling or utilizing for commercial purposes any product reproducing the design. it helps to increase the commercial value of a company as a registered industrial design constitutes a business asset. The owner can lease it for a period of time or sell it.

Can I register the design after selling it?

No, it will no longer be considered as a 'new' design if the design has been sold or made available to the public before the application for registration is made. Unless the applicant can sufficiently show to the satisfaction of the Registrar that the creator of the design could not have known that it had already been made available to the public.

What is the validity period after registration?

The registration of industrial design is valid for an initial 5years. It is renewable for two consecutive periods of 5years. Therefore, the total period any industrial design can be registered for protection is 15years.
