about us

Zavian Corporate Service Limited is a private limited liability company which provides top-notch business support, business advisory and corporate services to SMEs, Corporations and Organizations across several business sectors. We have a team of professionals who are committed to taking your business a notch higher from the point of conception of your business idea by advising you on the right legal structure that best suits your business idea to registration of your business and organization, preparation of your project plan, business plan and proposal, as well as ensuring that your intellectual property and brand is protected amongst other business support services. Through our blog-Be Legally Aware, we are committed to bridging the legal awareness gap by keeping individuals and businesses legally conscious as they engage in varieties of human and business activities. Ignorance of the law is really not an excuse.

Core Values

Zavian Corporate professionals operate on the core values of Integrity , Efficiency, Professionalism and Client Service Satisfaction.

Vision Statement

To be the most sought after business support and corporate service provider that operates with International Standard.

Mission Statement

To help businesses from the start to achieve their goals and take their business a notch-higher which will in turn have a positive ripple effect on the economy of Nigeria.
